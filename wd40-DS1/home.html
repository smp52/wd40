TOURS/GEAR/FUN

Located in the headwaters to the Everglades. The Paddling Center at Shingle Creek is located conveniently next to the attraction area just off US192. Experiences we offer include single and tandem kayaks, canoes, large stable paddle boards (SUP), biking, camping, and educational tours on a duffy 18 ft electric boat.

"This is a place that requires everyone to experience once in their life. Simply stunning how calm and peaceful this environment is. The facilities are clean and the staff is beyond professional. A must see!"
