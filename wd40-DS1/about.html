ABOUT

The Paddling Center was originally created to show the beauty and nature of the Florida area to both local and international visitors. Both seasoned and new outdoor enthusiasts can enjoy what we have to offer. Be it a simple family outing or a challenging trip, we have what you are looking for. We have been open a little over a year and in that time our offerings have expanded. We are Based out of Kissimee, River Shingle Creek.

Founders

Rich Miller:

Rich is a lifelong paddler and camping enthusiast, and has operated the premier Central Florida paddling tour company, Kissimmee River Kayak, LLC, for over 8 years. He was a founding member of the Osceola Blueways Committee formed to investigate establishing an extended paddling trail stretching from Shingle Creek to the Everglades, with adjacent hiking and biking trail and associated camp sites. When his two sons were young and wanting to ride off-road BMX bikes, Rich was instrumental in developing and managing what eventually became a highly successful and nationally recognized BMX bike track, located in St. Cloud, Florida, located just East of Kissimmee. Due to his vision of a potential Osceola Blueways trail and extensive tour guide experience, Rich will oversee paddling tour operations from individual rentals to medium and large groups.

John Jacobs (aka JJ or Dr. J):

John is recreational paddler, off-road mountain biker and for the past 4 years served as a support tour guide assisting Rich on selected tour excursions involving larger groups of paddlers. He is an Organizational Psychologist with over 20 years consulting experience helping small, medium, and large businesses/corporations and government agencies to implement effective business processes, to include developing and validating assessment and training instruments. John was a faculty member at the University of Central Florida's Institute for Simulation (UCF-IST) for 9 years and was awarded Researcher of the Year in 2000.
